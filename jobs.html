ASSISTANT
BOOKING
SUPPORT

Internship 32–40 hrs/week Min. 5 months Nootdorp, Netherlands

As an Assistant Booking Support intern at Deepbloe Agency, you'll play a vital supporting role at the intersection of talent, music and the global entertainment industry.

Your Responsibilities
  • —Supporting the management of artists' schedules, nationally and internationally
  • —Assisting in handling incoming booking requests
  • —Supporting negotiations for booking terms and conditions
  • —Aiding in the development of short and long-term strategies to expand artists' profiles
  • —Assisting with inbound and outbound sales and acquisitions
  • —Contributing to creating new business opportunities and maintaining industry contacts
  • —Supporting effective communication between the agency, artists and management
  • —Assisting in managing contracts and invoices
Your Profile
  • —Currently pursuing an HBO or university-level education
  • —Awareness of the latest developments in social media
  • —Excellent command of both Dutch and English, written and spoken
  • —Strong communication and commercial skills with a creative mindset
  • —Ability to work independently and take initiative
  • —Strong organizational skills and flexibility, including weekends
  • —No 9-to-5 mentality — this is the music industry
What We Offer
  • —A challenging and versatile internship within a young, driven team
  • —A dynamic and creative work environment with real responsibility
  • —Internship allowance
  • —Access to Deepbloe Agency events during your internship period
Important Information

This is a supporting internship role — not a full employment position.

Weekly hours: 32–40 hours

Duration: Minimum 5 months. Start date is flexible.
