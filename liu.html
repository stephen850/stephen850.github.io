About LIU

BRAZIL'S
GLOBAL
SOUND.

LIU is one of the most exciting names to emerge from Brazil's electronic music scene. A DJ and producer with a truly global footprint, he has built his reputation through relentless touring, distinctive productions and an ability to command any room — from intimate club floors to festival mainstages.

Ranked #79 in the DJ Mag Top 100 DJs — climbing 19 positions in a single year — LIU represents a new generation of Brazilian artists who are reshaping the international electronic music landscape. His fanbase voted him up with passion, and his live shows prove why: full rooms, peak-time energy, crowds with their hands in the air.

Confirmed for Tomorrowland Brasil 2025 (10–12 October, Itu – São Paulo), with headline appearances at Tomorrowland Belgium and S2O Songkran Festival already under his belt, LIU is a proven international draw — and one of Deepbloe's most in-demand artists globally.

The Sound

HOUSE.

LIU's DJ sets are built around a distinctive blend of house music, electronic energy and melodic sensibility. His programming moves fluidly between driving club tracks and peak-time festival anthems — always reading the room, always building the crowd.

As a producer, his studio work reflects the same versatility — original tracks that hold up both in intimate venues and on massive outdoor stages.
