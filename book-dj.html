WORLDWIDE.

Deepbloe is a global electronic music platform and DJ booking agency, representing world-class artists for clubs, festivals, brand experiences and private events. Whether you need a headliner for a 10,000-capacity festival or a resident DJ for a boutique club night, we handle every detail — from the initial offer to the final set.

What It Means to Book a DJ Through Deepbloe

When you book a DJ through Deepbloe, you're not dealing with an algorithm or a marketplace. You're working directly with a team that knows the artists personally, understands the market, and has placed acts on stages from Tomorrowland to Marquee Singapore to Bootshaus.

We make the process simple. You tell us what you need — the date, the venue, the vibe, the budget. We advise on the right artist, handle the negotiation, manage the contract, and support the show advance from start to finish.

Who We Book

Our roster spans house, techno, breaks, bass and everything in between. From established international acts with DJ Mag rankings to fast-rising artists building their global profile, the Deepbloe roster is curated for quality and fit — not volume.

How to Request a Booking

Use the form on our contact page and select "Promoter or Festival — talent buying" from the dropdown. Include your date, venue, and the artists you're considering. We respond within 48 hours.

For urgent requests or multi-show deals, email us directly at info@deepbloe.com.
