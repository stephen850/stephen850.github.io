About

Vinne is a Brazilian electronic music artist whose energetic and crowd-focused sets have earned him a reputation as one of the most reliable and exciting bookings in his market. With a sound that draws on the best of contemporary house and electronic music — and a natural ability to read and move a crowd — Vinne brings the kind of consistent energy that promoters and venues can count on.
