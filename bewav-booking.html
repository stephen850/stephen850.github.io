About

With supports from Solardo, Tim Baresko, Classmatic & Cloonee, bewav — written in lowercase — carries a TECH HOUSE sound worthy of capslock. The 29-year-old producer has gained prominence, especially in the Brazilian Rio scene.

His main influence comes from the percussive and groovy Tech House. He has as a sound reference coming from Spain, Argentina and the United Kingdom. "I have always enjoyed the percussive Tech House by Classmatic, Cloonee, Iglesias and Late Replies... The style draws attention by bringing a unique and full of groove to the dancefloor." His favorite labels are: Solid Grooves, Revival NY & Hellbent.

Outside of the pickups, the DJ and Producer still commands the young Rio label Clubinho, whose main objective is to foster the underground market, democratizing some of the electronic aspects, among them the underground Tech House.
